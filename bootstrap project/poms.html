All About Pomeranians

Pomeranians have been described as the ideal companion. They are playful, lively, smart, energetic, curious, bold, and sassy. They also have a very big-dog attitude despite their small size. Pomeranians are known for their personality but they are also known for their long and shiny coat and fox-like face. They are classified as a toy breed dog due to their small size. However, they are the most popular toy breed.
